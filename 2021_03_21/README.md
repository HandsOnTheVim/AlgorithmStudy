### 2. [개미굴](https://www.acmicpc.net/problem/14725)

> - 예제입력
>
>   ```
>   3
>   2 B A
>   4 A B C D
>   2 A C
>   
>   // answer
>   A
>   --B
>   ----C
>   ------D
>   --C
>   B
>   --A
>   ```
>
>   ```
>   4
>   2 KIWI BANANA
>   2 KIWI APPLE
>   2 APPLE APPLE
>   3 APPLE BANANA KIWI
>   
>   // answer
>   APPLE
>   --APPLE
>   --BANANA
>   ----KIWI
>   KIWI
>   --APPLE
>   --BANANA
>   ```
